About Us

Learn about our story and the technology behind our services

Our Story

We are an independent service provider founded by Vlad Gheorghe, in close collaboration with VFX industry advisors. As a data engineer with experience at large companies, I recognized that my technical skills could help solve common problems in the VFX industry through specialized tools and AI services.

Through partnerships with industry professionals who shared their challenges, I've developed solutions to save VFX producers, supervisors and coordinators countless hours on repetitive tasks, while improving accuracy and consistency across their workflows.

We are fully independent, not affiliated with any VFX studio or production company, ensuring complete confidentiality and neutrality.

Mission

We're on a mission to streamline VFX production workflows by automating time-consuming tasks, allowing creative professionals to focus their energy on what matters most: creating stunning visual effects.

Our tools are designed to help VFX companies of all sizes work more efficiently, accurately, and competitively in an increasingly demanding industry.

Values

  • Accuracy First: We prioritize precision in our technology, continuously refining our solutions to ensure you get reliable results
  • Time is Valuable: We respect that your time is precious and design our services to maximize efficiency
  • Client Confidentiality: Your data is treated with the utmost security and confidentiality
  • Continuous Improvement: We never stop refining our technology to deliver better results
