Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you process my script?

Standard processing time is at most 3 working days, and we send your parsed script immediately when ready. We're working to reduce processing times even further. For urgent needs, our Rush Processing service guarantees delivery within 24 hours.

Can you handle non-English scripts?

Yes! We can process scripts in multiple languages and even provide English translations as an add-on service.

How secure is my script data?

We take security seriously. All scripts are processed in a secure environment, and we're happy to sign NDAs before receiving your content.

Do you offer discounts for multiple scripts?

Yes, we provide volume pricing for production companies with multiple scripts. Contact us for a custom quote.
